Cookies come in two flavors: "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even after you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish as soon as you close your web browser. Poof!

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for these reasons:

-   **Necessary / Essential Cookies**

    -   **Managed by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely vital for getting you the services available through the Website and letting you use its cool features. They're super helpful for authenticating users and keeping those pesky fraudulent account uses at bay. Without these Cookies, the services you're asking for just can't be provided, and we only use them to make sure you get those services.

-   **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Managed by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies help us figure out if users have already given the thumbs-up to using cookies on the Website.

-   **Functionality Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Managed by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are like a little memory jog for us, allowing us to recall the choices you make while using the Website ‚Äì like your login deets or language preferences.
